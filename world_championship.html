WORLD CHESS CHAMPIONSHIP

WHERE EVERYTHING WILL BE DECIDED

THE FINAL STEP BEFORE THE SUMMIT

The World Chess Championship is the biggest match in Chess, where a single challenger gets the chance to challenge the reigning World Champion, with the title in wager.

The match is scheduled to be between the current World Champion, Magnus Carlsen, and the winner of the 2022 Candidates Tournament. However a few days after the previous championship in 2021, Carlsen said he may not be motivated enough to play another world championship match, and might not compete in the 2023 edition.

If Carlsen decides not to play, and if the match has the same conditions as the 2021 match, then the top two finishers in the 2022 Candidates will play a match for the World Championship
